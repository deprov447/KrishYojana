Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Under this scheme, farmers are provided crop insurance at a low premium.
To provide insurance coverage and financial support to the farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crop as a result of natural calamities, pests & diseases.There will be a uniform premium of only 2% to be paid by farmers for all Kharif crops and 1.5% for all Rabi crops. In case of annual commercial and horticultural crops, the premium to be paid will be only 5%.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.
  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account) .
  • Multi-tasking staff, Class IV and Group D government employees are eligible.
Documents required:
  • A Photo of the farmer
  • Farmer's ID card (PAN Card, Driving License, Voter ID Card, Passport, Aadhaar Card)
  • Address Proof of the farmer (driving license, voter ID card, passport, Aadhaar card)
  • If the field is owned by you, then keep the ‘Khasra’ paper and number/account number together.
  • If the crop is sown in the field, the proof has to be presented.
  • As proof of this, farmers need to get a letter written from people like Patwari, Sarpanch, Pradhan, Gaon Pradhan, etc.
